RTS Game

Instructions:

  • Start: Click the 'Start' button to begin the game. You'll start with 3 gatherers.
  • Gatherers: These units automatically collect resources. They will find the nearest resource, gather it, and bring it back to the base.
  • Soldiers: These are your combat units. Currently, they just spawn next to the base.
  • Resources: Resources are scattered around the map. They are essential for spawning more units. There are four types: Wood, Stone, Gold, and Food.
  • Controls: Use W, A, S, D keys to move the map view.
  • Objective: Gather as many resources as possible and spawn units to grow your army!
  • Spawn Requirements:
    • Gatherer: 4 Food.
    • Soldier: Requires 5 of each resource (Wood, Stone, Gold, and Food).
